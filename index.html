About My Education

I went to school at Garth Hill College in Bracknell. While I was there I studied Business , Physical Education (P.e), IT, History, English, Maths and Science. Due to my Ill health in my last year at school I was only able to take my three core subjects, English, Maths and Science, of which I passed all three. I was never the smartest at school but I would always put 100% effort in and try my best even if I did not understand I would always try before asking for help.
